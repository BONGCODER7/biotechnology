Who We Are

A Legacy of Scientific
Excellence at Pusa

The College of Basic Sciences and Humanities (CBSH) was established at Dr. Rajendra Prasad Central Agricultural University (formerly RAU), Pusa in November 1981 with the objective to strengthen teaching and research in basic sciences integral to agriculture.

RPCAU is an Institution of National Importance, Government of India. CBSH houses four dynamic departments offering M.Sc. (Ag.) and Ph.D. programmes, each at the cutting edge of agri-science research — from molecular breeding to AI-based genomic modelling.

Academic Programmes

Our Departments & Thrust Areas

Four specialized departments offering M.Sc. (Ag.) and Ph.D. programmes in agricultural basic sciences.

🧬
01

Agricultural Biotechnology & Molecular Biology

M.Sc. (Ag.) / Ph.D.

  • Molecular breeding for bio-fortification & stress management
  • Gene editing for quality improvement in oilseeds & pulses
  • Molecular speed breeding for climate-resilient genotypes
  • Micropropagation of medicinal & ornamental plants
Bioinformatics Gene Editing Speed Breeding
🦠
02

Microbiology

M.Sc. (Ag.) / Ph.D.

  • Green Microbiology: Biofertilizers & biocontrol agents
  • Organic & natural farming through microbial aspects
  • Applied Microbiology: Enzymes, pigments & acids production
  • Germplasm conservation & microbial diversity exploration
Biocontrol Food Microbiology Natural Farming
🌿
03

Botany, Plant Physiology & Biochemistry

M.Sc. (Ag.) / Ph.D.

  • Morpho-physiological mechanisms of abiotic stress tolerance
  • Metabolomics of plant stress response & biochemical pathways
  • Biochemistry during storage to extend shelf life
  • Essential oils from agricultural waste & their applications
Metabolomics Abiotic Stress Essential Oils
📊
04

Statistics & Computer Applications

M.Sc. (Ag.) / Ph.D.

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning in Agriculture
  • Big Data Analysis & Data Science
  • Artificial Neural Networks & Simulation models
  • Genomic modelling & Experimental design for field crops
AI & ML Big Data Genomic Modelling
